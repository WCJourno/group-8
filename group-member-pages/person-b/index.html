Find Out About Nia

Nia Coats has been working as a freelance journalist since 2021, and in that same time, she began releasing print magazines that highlight the amazing music scene here in the Bay Area. In 2024, she started her Master's Program at the Uc Berkeley School of Journalism and that same year she released the foruth issue of her magazine covering the theme of sound. 2024 was a big year of firsts for Nia, that same year she also began her hobby as a radio DJ for the local online radio station HYDE FM.
