About My Club

Growing up in a family of small business owners sparked my interest in entrepreneurship and the challenges that come with building and sustaining a business. Seeing that environment up close made me want to find ways to support small businesses and help them grow. At UC Berkeley, I'm currently building Roots Consulting, a pro bono student consulting group that partners with local businesses to help them solve real challenges while giving students meaningful, hands-on experience working directly with entrepreneurs.
